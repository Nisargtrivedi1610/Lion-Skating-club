Nepal Compition

We are thrilled to announce a momentous victory for Nepal in its inaugural international skating competition! Our talented skating team not only participated in the global event hosted in Nepal but emerged triumphant, securing a remarkable win. This achievement not only reflects the skill and dedication of our athletes but also signifies a historic milestone for Nepali sports. As we bask in the glory of this success, we look forward to inspiring a new era of competitive skating in Nepal and fostering a culture of excellence in sports on the international stage. Join us in celebrating this proud moment as we continue to strive for greatness in the world of skating.
